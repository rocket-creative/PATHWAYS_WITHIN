## Why Whiten Your Teeth?

**Teeth become stained from:**
- Coffee and tea
- Red wine
- Tobacco use
- Aging (enamel thins, revealing darker dentin)
- Certain medications
- Excessive fluoride
- Trauma
- Dark-colored foods and drinks

**While staining isn't dangerous,** it can seriously impact your confidence.

---

## Our Professional Whitening

**In-office whitening delivers dramatic results** you can't achieve with over-the-counter products.

**The process:**
1. Protective barrier applied to lips and gums
2. Professional-strength whitening gel applied to teeth
3. Blue LED light activates whitening gel
4. Light and gel work together to break down stains
5. Treatment lasts 15-45 minutes
6. Results: 3-12 shades lighter

**Professional vs. Over-the-Counter:**
- Stronger whitening agents (faster results)
- Controlled application (safer for gums)
- Visible results in one session
- Supervised by professionals
