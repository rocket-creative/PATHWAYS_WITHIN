### How often should I get HydraFacial?

**For optimal results: Every 4-6 weeks**

**Why monthly?**  
Your skin renews itself every 28 days. Monthly HydraFacials support this natural cycle, keeping skin clear, hydrated, and glowing.

**Adjust based on:**
- Your skin type (oily skin may benefit from more frequent)
- Your concerns (acne or anti-aging may need different schedules)
- Your budget and goals

**Our estheticians will recommend** the ideal schedule for you.
