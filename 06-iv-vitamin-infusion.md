## What is IV Vitamin Infusion?

**IV vitamin infusion is a wellness treatment** that delivers vitamins, minerals, amino acids, and other nutrients directly into your bloodstream through an intravenous drip.

**Why bypass the digestive system?**

When you take oral vitamins:
- They must pass through your digestive tract
- Stomach acid breaks them down
- Only 20-50% of nutrients are actually absorbed
- Effects take hours or days to notice

**With IV infusion:**
- Nutrients go straight to your bloodstream
- 100% absorption (nothing is lost to digestion)
- Higher concentrations can be safely administered
- Effects are often felt within 24-48 hours
- Immediate hydration benefits

**This makes IV therapy significantly more effective** than taking vitamins orally, especially when you need rapid results.

## What to Expect During Your Session

### Before Your Infusion

**Preparation:**
- Eat a light meal before arriving (don't come on an empty stomach)
- Hydrate well beforehand
- Wear comfortable clothing with sleeves that roll up easily
- Arrive 5-10 minutes early for paperwork (first visit)

**Brief consultation:**
- We'll discuss your goals and health history
- Choose the blend that best fits your needs
- Answer any questions you have

---

### During Your Infusion

**Comfort is key:**
- Relax in a comfortable reclining chair
- Bring a book, headphones, or just relax
- Soft lighting and calming environment
- A trained healthcare provider inserts the IV (small needle in your arm)
- The infusion drips slowly over 30-60 minutes

**What you'll feel:**
- A slight pinch when the IV is inserted
- Cool sensation as fluids enter your bloodstream
- Deep relaxation (many people close their eyes and rest)
- Sometimes a mild taste or smell from certain vitamins (normal)

---

### After Your Infusion

**Immediate effects:**
- Improved hydration (you'll likely need to use the restroom!)
- Sense of wellbeing and calm
- Some people feel energized immediately
- Others notice effects within 24-48 hours

**Post-infusion care:**
- Drink plenty of water
- Eat normally
- Resume regular activities (no downtime!)
- Notice how you feel over the next few days

**Results typically:**
- Peak within 24-48 hours
- Last several days to weeks
- Improve with regular treatments

### Are there any side effects?

**IV vitamin infusions are generally well-tolerated with minimal side effects.**

**Possible minor side effects:**
- Slight bruising at injection site (common, harmless)
- Cool sensation during infusion
- Taste or smell from vitamins
- Increased urination (from hydration)
- Mild fatigue (rare, usually passes quickly)

**Serious side effects are very rare** when administered properly.

**To minimize bruising:**
- Stay well-hydrated before treatment
- Avoid blood thinners if possible
- Apply pressure to site after IV removal
