## Understanding Trauma
Trauma has lasting physical and emotional effects that can leave you tired, jumpy, anxious, feeling ashamed, exposed, insecure, and wondering who you can trust. We can't control everything that happens to us, or how these events affect us. However, we can help you manage trauma and reduce symptoms.

## What is Trauma?
Many experience trauma after intense experiences affecting mental, behavioral, and emotional wellbeing. Trauma can live with you for life, affecting you below the surface and manifesting through personality, actions, and thoughts.
