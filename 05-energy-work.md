## Reiki: Universal Life Force Energy

### What is Reiki?

**Reiki is a Japanese healing technique** (developed by Dr. Mikao Usui in the early 1900s) that uses universal life force energy to promote healing and balance in the body.

**The word "Reiki" means:**
- Rei = Universal, spiritual wisdom
- Ki = Life force energy (similar to Chi or Prana)

**During Reiki, the practitioner channels healing energy** through their hands to the client, either with gentle touch or hovering just above the body. This energy goes where it's needed most, supporting your body's natural healing wisdom.
